## 📖 Using the application

Start the app from your start menu. You will see a login screen. Enter your Apple ID credentials to connect your account. The application uses a secure connection to talk to Apple servers. Your password stays private and the app does not store your authentication details permanently.

Once logged in, you see your music library displayed in a list. Select the albums or individual songs you want to save. Use the download button to start the process. The progress bar shows the status of each download. Your files save to a designated music folder on your computer. You can change this location in the settings menu.

## 🛡️ Privacy and security

Your privacy matters. This application acts as a bridge between your computer and the music service. It handles your requests locally. We do not track your listening habits. All data transfers use encrypted protocols. 

If you want to clear your data, you can sign out at any time. Signing out removes your session tokens from the local system cache. We recommend that you keep your operating system updated to ensure the best security standards.

## 🔧 Frequently asked questions

**Does this app store my music permanently?**
Yes. Once the download finishes, the file exists on your hard drive. You own that copy.

**Can I move these files to my phone?**
Yes. Since the files save to your local folder, you can copy them to any device that supports your chosen file format.

**Why does the app ask for my password?**
The app needs to prove your identity to the music service. This allows it to see the library that belongs to your paid account.

**What happens if a download fails?**
Check your internet connection first. If the problem continues, restart the app and try the download again. Large playlists can take time to process.
